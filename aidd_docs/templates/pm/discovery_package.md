## Interview Guide (Jobs-To-Be-Done)

**Target interviewees**: [Profile / segment]
**Duration**: [30-45 min]

### Opening (2 min)

> "Tell me about your role and how you spend most of your day."

### Situation mapping (10 min)

1. When [context], what are you trying to accomplish?
2. Walk me through the last time you had to [relevant task]. What happened?
3. What triggered that situation?

### Problem depth (15 min)

4. What makes [task/situation] difficult today?
5. What have you tried to solve it? What worked, what didn't?
6. [Hypothesis H1] → "How do you currently handle [X]?"
7. [Hypothesis H2] → "How important is [Y] to you, really?"
8. What would a perfect solution look like?

### Closing (5 min)

9. Is there anything you wish I had asked?
10. Who else should I talk to?
